About

Researcher.

Building companies, systems, and knowledge at the intersection of AI, cybersecurity, and quantum computing.

Anshum Srivastava is the founder of Progeta Technologies and a systems engineer working at the intersection of AI, cybersecurity, and quantum computing. He builds companies, does research, and teaches — because the most consequential problems at this boundary require all three.

He has shipped over 20 technical projects, taught 1,000+ students across workshops and academic programs, contributed to applied defense research at DRDO, and authored research in quantum cryptography and cybersecurity currently under peer review.

His products — SelfOS and InnerCircle — are live tools used by real people, built on cognitive science, reinforcement learning, and behavioral design.

What drives the work

"To design systems — technical and human — that make people better at thinking, learning, and deciding. Not just faster at executing."

Engineering Principles
1
Think in systems, not silos. Every meaningful problem is a set of interacting parts. The interesting work is always in the interfaces.
2
Security is a foundation, not a feature. Resilience has to be designed in from day one — not bolted on after the breach.
3
Learning is the real infrastructure. The best tools don't just automate tasks — they sharpen the person using them.
4
Ship, measure, refine. Research earns its value only when it becomes something real people can stress-test.
5
Clarity is a technical skill. Explaining a complex system simply is as valuable as the ability to build it.
